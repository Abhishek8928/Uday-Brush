Professional paint brush characteristics

The brush should be full.A good paint brush has a substantial thickness relative to its width and is thickly filled with bristles.
The bristles should belong,longer than the width of the brush except for extremely wide brushes.
3rd points needs to be changed ( A taper of shap….) to - Good bristles have bend recovery. Hold a new brush, bend back the bristles and let them go. they should snap back straight in a spring like motion.
The bristle should be tipped or flagged. This gives a soft,Smooth feeling when the tips of the bristle are stroked accross the palm of the hand.
